Do I need one?

A legal requirement for almost every premises

Under the Regulatory Reform (Fire Safety) Order 2005, a fire risk assessment is legally required for all non-domestic premises — including workplaces, holiday lets and the common parts of residential buildings. As the responsible person, choosing a competent professional ensures a thorough evaluation of hazards, helps you comply with the law, and provides the evidence insurers increasingly ask for. Not sure whether your building needs one? Ask us — it costs nothing to check.
