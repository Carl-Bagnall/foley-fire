Fire safety consultancy

Practical, regulation-led fire safety advice for projects at any stage — from early design to occupation.

Advice when and where you need it

We provide practical fire safety consultancy for projects at any stage, from early design through to completion and occupation. We can determine appropriate fire alarm categories, advise where fire doors are required, confirm fire door ratings and review means of escape arrangements.

We also support with compartmentation queries, layout changes, change-of-use considerations and compliance clarification. If you need a competent opinion before submitting to Building Control, progressing works or signing off a project, we provide clear and proportionate guidance.

Early input prevents costly rework

We regularly work alongside architects, developers, managing agents and Building Control to ensure fire safety requirements are considered properly and implemented correctly. Early input often prevents costly remedial works and delays later in the project.

From one question to ongoing support

Whether you have a single technical question or require ongoing project support, we offer dependable, commercially realistic advice grounded in current legislation and recognised guidance.

Ongoing fire safety advice is included as part of our fire risk assessment service, and we remain available to support you beyond the initial report.

What we advise on

  • Fire alarm categories and detection strategy
  • Fire door requirements and ratings
  • Means of escape arrangements
  • Compartmentation and layout changes
  • Change of use and compliance clarification

Who this is for: Architects · developers · managing agents · Building Control
