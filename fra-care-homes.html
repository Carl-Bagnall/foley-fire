Residential & nursing homes

Fire safety guidance tailored to complex residential care environments.

Experience with higher-risk premises

We specialise in fire risk assessments for residential care, nursing homes and supported living environments. From smaller homes to larger multi-occupancy settings, we bring the experience required to assess higher-risk premises with clarity and confidence.

Becky’s background in housing, combined with our experience across care environments, gives us practical insight into how these buildings operate day to day. Our assessments are proportionate, structured and tailored to resident dependency, staffing levels and evacuation strategy.

Experienced in care settings

We’ve supported a wide range of care providers in meeting their duties under fire safety legislation while maintaining a safe, well-managed environment. We understand the importance of progressive horizontal evacuation, compartmentation, fire doors and staff procedures in protecting vulnerable residents.

Our reports provide clear, achievable recommendations that strengthen life safety without unnecessary disruption.

Empathetic and solution-focused

Care environments require sensitivity as well as competence. We work collaboratively with managers and staff to identify practical, proportionate solutions that balance compliance with the wellbeing and dignity of residents — whether you manage a long-established nursing home or a newly registered service.

What we assess in care settings

  • Evacuation strategy and resident dependency
  • Progressive horizontal evacuation provision
  • Compartmentation and fire doors
  • Staff procedures and training arrangements
  • Night-time staffing considerations

Who this is for: Care providers · home managers · supported living operators
