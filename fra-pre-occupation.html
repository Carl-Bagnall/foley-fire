Pre-occupation & development

Practical, compliant fire safety guidance for architects and developers — before a building is brought into use.

Assessed before first occupation

We provide thorough pre-occupation fire risk assessments for offices, newly built or converted flats, holiday accommodation and multi-storey residential buildings. Whether you are preparing a property for first occupation or completing works prior to handover, we ensure your premises are assessed clearly and proportionately before they are brought into use.

For private residential buildings over two storeys where Building Control sign-off is required, we assess key life safety provisions including fire doors, compartmentation, means of escape, fire detection and emergency lighting.

Supporting safe occupation & Building Control approval

We understand the pressures that come with project completion and occupation deadlines. Our assessments are structured to support developers, architects and property owners in demonstrating compliance prior to occupation.

If remedial actions are required, we provide clear, proportionate recommendations aligned with current guidance and Building Regulations — helping you achieve a smooth sign-off process.

Practical guidance during redesign & conversion

If you are redesigning or converting a building — creating flats, office space or holiday lets — we can provide fire safety input at planning or pre-completion stage. Early advice often prevents costly alterations later: from fire door specification to layout considerations and means-of-escape strategy, we offer pragmatic support so life safety is embedded within your design.

What we assess pre-occupation

  • Fire doors and specification
  • Compartmentation and fire-stopping
  • Means of escape and travel distances
  • Fire detection and emergency lighting
  • Documentation for Building Control sign-off

Who this is for: Developers · architects · private owners pre-handover
