Listed & heritage buildings

Protecting life and preserving legacy — tailored assessments that respect your building’s character.

Specialists in protected properties

We specialise in fire risk assessments for listed and heritage buildings — from Grade I, II* and II properties to period homes, converted estates and historic venues. We bring a depth of experience that ensures your building is assessed with the care and sensitivity it deserves.

Managing a protected property carries unique responsibilities. Our assessments strike the right balance: clear, proportionate fire safety guidance that ensures compliance without compromising architectural integrity or character.

Trusted by conservation-conscious clients

We’ve supported a wide range of heritage property owners, from private estate managers to public trustees, helping them meet their legal obligations while preserving what makes their buildings special.

With a focus on life safety, we offer practical advice tailored to your building’s unique requirements — and we understand how to work within listed building constraints and alongside conservation officers.

Sensitively protecting what matters

Whether you’re responsible for a listed public space, a converted residence or a cherished family estate, we’re here to support you with expert, sensitive fire risk assessments that put people and preservation first.

What we consider in heritage properties

  • Escape routes within historic layouts
  • Detection and alarm proposals proportionate to the building
  • Compartmentation without harming historic fabric
  • Discreet signage and emergency lighting solutions
  • Listed building consent and conservation constraints

Who this is for: Estate managers · trustees · venue owners · period conversions
