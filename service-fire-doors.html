Fire door surveys

Competent fire door surveys with clear, actionable reporting.

Why fire doors matter

Fire doors play a critical role in protecting escape routes and maintaining compartmentation. If they fail, the integrity of the building’s entire fire strategy is compromised.

We carry out detailed fire door surveys across residential, commercial and multi-occupied buildings, assessing compliance with current legislation and recognised standards.

What each inspection covers

Each door is inspected for certification, condition, gaps, hinges, closers, seals, glazing, signage and overall suitability for its location. Where required, we confirm fire resistance ratings and identify whether doors are appropriate for the level of protection needed.

Recommendations are proportionate and practical, helping you understand exactly what is required to bring doors up to standard.

Supporting your legal duties

We regularly support managing agents, landlords, contractors and responsible persons in meeting their legal duties, including annual inspection requirements where applicable. Whether you require a standalone fire door survey or inspections alongside a fire risk assessment, we provide structured, dependable, regulation-led reporting.

What we inspect on every door

  • Certification and fire resistance rating
  • Gaps, hinges, closers and seals
  • Glazing and signage
  • Condition and overall suitability for location
  • Prioritised, practical remedial advice

Who this is for: Managing agents · landlords · contractors · responsible persons
